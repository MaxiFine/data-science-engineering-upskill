# Amalitech Sales Story: Financial data.csv

## Big Idea
Optimizing discounts and focusing on high-margin products can lift profit significantly without increasing spend.

## Audience
Sales & Finance leadership

## Slides

- Slide 1: Title & Big Idea
  - KPI cards: Total Sales, Total Profit, Margin %, Discount %

- Slide 2: Sales & Profit Trend
  - Line chart: Date (Month) vs Total Sales and Total Profit
  - Callouts: peak months, dips

- Slide 3: Margin % Over Time
  - Line chart: Date (Month) vs Margin %
  - Annotate periods with high discounts

- Slide 4: Discount Impact
  - Clustered column: Discount Band vs Discounts and Margin %
  - Insight: bands eroding margin

- Slide 5: Segment Performance
  - Bar chart: Segment vs Profit and Margin %
  - Identify high-margin segments

- Slide 6: Product Mix & Profitability
  - Combo chart: Product vs Gross Sales (columns) + Margin % (line)
  - Pareto cumulative line for profit concentration

- Slide 7: Country Comparison
  - Bar or map: Country vs Profit and Margin %
  - Opportunities by geography

- Slide 8: Seasonality
  - Column chart: Month Number vs Sales and Profit
  - Plan campaigns/capacity

- Slide 9: Price vs Units
  - Scatter: Sale Price (X), Units Sold (Y), size by Profit, color Product
  - Price elasticity hotspots

- Slide 10: Recommendations
  - Discount guardrails for specific bands/products
  - Focus on high-margin segments/products
  - Pricing experiments where units remain strong at higher price
  - Geo-specific playbooks for low-margin countries

- Slide 11: Next 30/90 Days
  - 30: Implement discount policy pilots; monitor Margin %
  - 90: Rebalance product focus; roll out successful pricing changes

- Slide 12: Risks & Mitigations
  - Risk: Over-tightening discounts; Mitigation: A/B by product elasticity
